Apple Beignets with Vanilla Rum Sobayon

A simple recipe for a French classic

Introduction

Apple beignets are a classic french desert that is simple to make. This recipe actually has a southern twist because it is made in the New Orleans style. The recipe takes about a half hour to prepare from start to finish.

You will need the following ingredients:

  • 3 apples
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 beer
  • 1 shot vanilla rum
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon
  • oil
  • 1 lb. butter

Instructions

For the Beignets:

  1. First peel and core the apples, then cut into rings 1/4 inch thick.
  2. Then mix half the beer, 1 egg and all th flour in a bowl until well combined. It is ok if there are some lumps.
  3. In a large frying pan heat the oil to 375 degrees.
  4. Dip the apple in the batter mixture and fry for 2 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  5. Combine 1/4 cup sugar with the cinnamon and lightly dust the beignets with the mixture.

For the Sauce:

  1. Melt the butter, separate the eggs
  2. Place the egg yolks in a bowl with the remaining 1/4 cup of sugar and whisk briskly.
  3. Slowly add in the melted butter while you continue to whisk until the mixture thickens.
  4. Add in the rum and taste for sweetness. More sugar can be added if needed.

To Serve:

Place a tablespoon of sauce on the plate and then place one apple ring on top. Plate can be garnished with cinnomon stick and mint if desired.
